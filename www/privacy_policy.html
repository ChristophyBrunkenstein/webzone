Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

1. Data Collected

Local Storage: The App stores non-personal data, such as your favorite recipes or settings, locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data stays on your device and is not collected or shared. Google AdMob: The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve personalized ads and analyze ad performance: Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID). IP address. Ad interactions (e.g., ad views or clicks). Approximate location (inferred from IP address or device settings). We do not directly collect or store any personal information.

2. How Data is Used

Local data is used to save your preferences or progress in the App (e.g., favorite recipes). AdMob uses data to provide personalized ads and improve ad performance, as managed by Google.

3. Data Sharing

Local data stored on your device is not shared with anyone. Data collected by AdMob is shared with Google and its advertising partners for ad delivery and analytics. For details, see Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local data is stored securely on your device and not transmitted. AdMob’s data is handled by Google under their security and privacy practices.

5. Your Choices

You can clear local data by uninstalling the App or clearing the App’s data in the app. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device’s advertising settings (e.g., Google’s Ad Settings).

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at StrugglerCookbook@proton.me
